What Do I Like To Do For Fun!?

Well, I Watch An Awful Lot of MMA/UFC

Hands down my favorite past time.

I've been into mixed martial arts for many years, mainly becuase I think it's cool and admirable. That might sound crazy but, I don't know, I guess there's just something about two grown adults competeing in fair combat that is unlike anything else. I don't really watch other sports but I can easily watch the full UFC fight card on a Saturdays if I'm not working. Even if I am working, chances are I'm watching the fights on my phone or tablet or something. I'm sitting here thinking about it, and yea, sometimes fight cards have 10+ fights scheduled for one night, and I'll watch all of them. I can appreciate where dicipline meets art. I like analyzing the striking, defense, head movement, countering, fainting, kicking, timing of strikes, there's just so much that goes into combat sports its crazy. Plus I love a good highlight K.O. Needless to say, I watch most of the UFC events from start to finish, which can sometimes span 4-6 hours, most weekends. So that's probably my main hobby.

So Why Do I Want To Be A Developer?

Many reasons, here's the gist.

I have always been a fan of online businesses such as ecommerce sites, dropshipping stores, and follow a lot of tech entreprenuers online. I have tried to play around here and there with building websites, but nothing like this. I've used wix, weebly, shopify, and wordpress in the past to make litte sites here and there for various endevours. I thought those drag and drop website building platforms were cool, and I just recently started to realize how complex coding a whole site from scratch actually is. I wanted to become a developer because I think AI is at a point where if you do not adapt to it now, or learn how to use it for financial gain, or at least know how to work with it, you will be at a major disatvantage. I think joining this course is a great place to start. I will gain the fundamentals of how coding works, and then I can just keep learning other languages, take other courses, so on and so forth because it is my belief, that those that do not do what they can to learn how things are changing, will be left behind. Maybe not, but I'm here primarily because of that. And because I want to change industries and careers. I've been doing insurance for almost a decade, and I'd rather switch now to a different industry, so that in the next decade, I can be better positioned.
